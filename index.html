Your biggest differentiator: Competitors force clients into one system. You walk in and say "we work around your business, not the other way around." That alone closes deals others lose.
1
Conditional Call Forwarding
Their existing number stays exactly the same. Nothing changes about how they use their phone. When they don't answer in 3–4 rings, the call automatically forwards to Vapi. Customers never know anything changed.
Zero disruptionKeeps existing numberEasiest setup
2
Dedicated 918 Vapi Number
A clean dedicated Tulsa 918 area code number for all business calls. Put it on their website, truck wrap, Google Business Profile. Their personal cell stays private. All business calls go to the AI — only qualified leads get forwarded to them.
ProfessionalPersonal/business split~$2/mo
3
After Hours Only
Recommended for trial
Business hours — phone rings normally. After 5pm, weekends, job site — AI takes over automatically. Catches every call they were already missing anyway. Zero change to daytime workflow. Best for skeptical clients and the free trial.
Best for free trialEasiest sellNo daytime disruption

Set up a free Google Voice number, point it to Vapi, update their Google Business Profile. Most trade leads come from Google searches — this captures every single one. Their real number never touched. Free to set up.

Onboarding
// From signed to live — step by step
Onboarding Steps
1
Discovery Call
Get their numbers — missed calls/week, avg job value, team size, pain points. This becomes your build brief. Come back to Claude with those answers and the Vapi script gets written same day.
2
Pick Phone Setup Option
Walk them through all 5 options (see Phone Setup tab). Let them choose what fits. Most new clients pick Option 3 (after hours only) to start — lowest risk, easiest yes.
3
Team Roster (if Option 5)
If they pick smart routing, collect names, roles, and contact numbers for everyone the AI needs to know — sales, tech, maintenance. One person per role minimum.
4
Build the Agent
Claude writes the script tailored to their niche. You paste it into Vapi. ElevenLabs voice selected. GHL webhook connected. Takes one afternoon.
5
Set Up Call Forwarding
Based on their chosen option. Usually takes 5–15 minutes. Carrier settings or a short dial code. Walk them through it on the phone if needed.
6
Test — 5 Calls Minimum
Call the number yourself as different types of callers. Walk through every scenario. Tweak the script until it sounds completely natural. Don't go live until it passes.
7
Go Live
Flip the switch. Client gets a confirmation text. You both watch the first real calls come in. Check GHL to confirm leads are logging correctly.
Discovery Questions
→ How many calls do you get per day?
→ How many do you miss after hours or on job sites?
→ What's your average job worth?
→ Biggest pain — missed calls, no-shows, tire kickers?
→ How many people are on your team?
→ Do you have someone dedicated to sales?
→ What do you want the AI to say when it answers?
→ What questions should it ask every caller?
